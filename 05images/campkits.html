Camping Kits

Basic Car Camping Kit

Car camping is a great way for beginners to enjoy the outdoors without needing expensive gear or hiking long distances. With your vehicle nearby, you can bring along more comforts and still have everything you need for a relaxing weekend in nature. This kit covers the essential items you need.

Tent & Sleeping Gear
Includes a 3-season tent, sleeping bag rated for cool nights, and a sleeping pad or air mattress for comfort.
Cooking Essentials
A simple propane stove, lightweight cookware, reusable utensils, and a cooler for food storage.
Lighting & Safety
Bring a headlamp or lantern for visibility, and a small first aid kit in case of minor injuries.

With these essentials packed, you’ll be well-prepared for your car camping adventure. As you gain more experience, you can gradually add gear that fits your personal comfort and style. The goal is to keep things simple and stress-free—so you can spend less time worrying about gear and more time enjoying the outdoors.

Lightweight Backpacking Kit

Essential Gear to Keep It Light

  • Lightweight tent or bivy sack
  • Compact sleeping bag
  • Inflatable sleeping pad
  • Single-burner backpacking stove
  • Collapsible pot and cup
  • Spork or multi-use utensil
  • Freeze-dried meals or trail mix
  • Water bottle or hydration bladder
  • Water filter or purification tablets
